What Qualifications Does It Take to Work With Us?

 Being a Web Developer here at Hot Beans, you will need:

  •   • Background knowledge in HTML, CSS and JavaScript (2 years)
  •   • Previous work experience in a web development company (2 years)
  •   • Be able to work as a team and to tackle any problem given
  •   • Have a Strong work ethic
  •   • Have a passion for web development
 

  And to take on the role of web developer here at Hot Beans, a Level 3 qualification in a computer related degree is required.
